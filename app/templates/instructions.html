Instructions

  1. Use a high-resolution smartphone camera
  2. Maintain a distance of 15cm from the camera
  3. Use standard light settings
  4. Capture frontal and lateral views
  5. Upload the images to the app
  6. Results are based on Global Acne Grading System (GAGS)
