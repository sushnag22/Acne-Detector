Instructions to be followed for better results.

  1. Use a high-resolution smartphone camera: Use an iOS or android smartphone camera with higher resolution to capture the images. This will ensure that the image quality is good and the details are clear.
  2. Maintain a distance of 15cm from the camera: While capturing the images, make sure to maintain a distance of approximately 15cm between the camera and the face. This will help in capturing the image without any distortions or blurs.
  3. Use standard light settings: It is recommended to capture the images with standard light settings. This will ensure that the images are captured uniformly and the results are accurate.
  4. Check lighting conditions and distance during image capturing: The mobile application used for capturing images should check the lighting conditions and the distance between the user's face and the smartphone camera. This will help in capturing the images with the correct lighting conditions and distance.
  5. Capture frontal and lateral views: Capture three images of the face, including frontal, right lateral, and left lateral views. Make sure to capture lesions on the forehead, nose, chin, and cheeks, as per the instructions given.
  6. Upload the images to the web application: Once you have captured the images, upload them to the acnegrade web application. Make sure to upload high-quality images with clear details to obtain better results.
  7. Results are based on Global Acne Grading System (GAGS): Keep in mind that the results obtained will be based on the Global Acne Grading System (GAGS). This system assigns a grade to the acne based on the severity of the condition, so make sure to provide accurate and clear images to get the most accurate results.
